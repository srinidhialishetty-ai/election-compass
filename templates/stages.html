Stages

Read the election process as a clean sequence of stages.

This page is for users who prefer structured reading before asking the assistant for clarification or follow-up detail.

01

Preparation / Pre-Election Context

Authorities organize the election, announce procedures, and prepare the conditions needed for participation.

 02

Registration / Eligibility Context

Eligible voters confirm whether they can participate and whether any deadlines or requirements apply.

 03

Campaign / Candidate Phase

People gather information, compare options, and follow official notices before ballots are cast.

 04

Voting Day

Voters complete the official process to cast ballots, often including check-in and instructions.

 05

Counting and Results

Votes are counted and reported before official results are finalized through the proper process.

 06

Post-Election Outcome

Final certification or confirmation closes the process and formally records the result.
